Beloved

(Book Cover Design)

Recently I was working on a book cover project about a story inspired by a its author, Tony Morrison, about a former slave in Cincinnati, Ohio.

I used my best imagination to re-create her portrait. The birds represent her desire of becoming freedom. sketches were made on different formats that I had to process with Illustration and Photoshop to adapt them to the format that I chose for the book.
