Festival Poster

(Music Poster Design)

"The Playlist Orlando Festival Poster" is a school project of making an advertising poster for the event. It based on the theme of its up-beat music. I took some inspiration from DJ music poster, holiday festival, and some EDM music vibe.

For this project, I went with the glitch effect with a design of a play button for its main image to mix with a dark modern color scheme. This illustration style exceptionally a movement effect. The color is contrast with its dark background, so it gonna be light up to make it eye-catching in the dark. Overall, my goal is to make it suitable with all the songs that have in this event.
